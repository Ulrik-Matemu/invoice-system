Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 31 Dec 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how myInvo we collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our invoice management services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide invoice creation and management services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your email and password provided during authentication via Firebase.
  • Invoice Data: Details you input to create invoices, including client names, addresses, descriptions of services, and amounts. This data is stored in our Firebase backend.
  • Financial Information: We do not process or store credit card details. All subscriptions and payments are handled securely through the Google Play Billing System.
  • Usage Data: Basic technical information (e.g., device type, operating system) collected automatically to ensure app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To save, retrieve, and manage your invoices and client list.
  • Subscription Management: To verify your "Pro" status and provide unlimited invoice features.
  • Service Improvement: To troubleshoot bugs and improve the user experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

We use Google Firebase to store your data. Your information is protected using industry-standard security measures provided by Google, including encryption in transit and at rest. While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share data with the following service providers to make the app work:

  • Google Firebase: For data storage and user authentication.
  • Google Play Store: To process in-app purchases and subscriptions.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may delete your invoices or your entire account at any time through the App settings. Upon account deletion, all associated invoice and client data will be permanently removed from our active databases.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can perform most of these actions directly within the App. For further assistance, contact us at the email below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
